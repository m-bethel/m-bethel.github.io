Our Vision

Core Platform

01

Democratic Reform

Strengthen democratic institutions through ranked-choice voting, campaign finance reform, and ending gerrymandering. Every vote should count equally.

02

Government Transparency

Mandate open-source government systems, public legislative records, and real-time budget tracking. Democracy dies in darkness.

03

Individual Liberty

Protect personal freedoms, privacy rights, and bodily autonomy. Government exists to serve citizens, not control them.

04

Economic Sovereignty

Support local economies, worker cooperatives, and fair taxation. Economic power should be distributed, not concentrated.

05

Environmental Stewardship

Balance economic growth with sustainable practices. Protect natural resources for future generations while respecting property rights.

06

Education & Opportunity

Invest in accessible education, vocational training, and lifelong learning. Knowledge is the foundation of sovereignty.
